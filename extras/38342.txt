Kurt Schwitters, Hannover, Germany; Lysaker, Norway; (then possibly in England). 1929 – 1948 Ernst Schwitters (the artist’s son), Lysaker near Oslo, Norway. Inherited KS estate, 1948 - 1972 Marlborough Fine Arts, London. On consignment, 1963 - 1972 Meshulam Riklis, New York / McCrory Corporation. Purchase, 1972 – 1983 The Museum of Modern Art, New York. The Riklis Collection of McCrory Corporation, 1983
